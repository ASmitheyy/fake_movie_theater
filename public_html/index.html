    About Page

    This theater has been remodified by Bishnu Adhikari. Simpson Movie Theater is located in Indianola, Iowa on Simpson's campus. The Simpson Movie Theatre keeps up on all of the latest movies of every genre. Simpson Movies have 5 movies currenly in the box office, a comedy, action, animated, romance and horror movie. Matinee prices are $5. Evening prices are $7. On weekends Simpson students recieve discounted tickets for evening movies upon signing up for alerted notifications.
