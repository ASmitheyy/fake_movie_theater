Simpson Movie Theater

This theater was made by Khrystina and Justin. Simpson Movie Theater is located in Indianola, Iowa on Simpson's campus. The Simpson Movie Theater keeps up on all of the latest movies of every genre.

Simpson Movies have 5 movies currently in the box office, a comedy, action, animated, romance and horror movie.

Matinee prices are $5. Evening prices are $7. On weekends Simpson students receive discounted tickets for evening movies upon signing up for alerted notifications.
