Tide Pod Man

Paul Nerf (Ryan Reynolds) was just an ordinary guy... until he swallowed a Tide Pod. Discovering endless powers and abilities, Paul is being targeted by the worlds scariest assassins

and can only be saved by eating more Tide Pods to strengthen his abilites. The problem is, with every Pod he eats, the weaker his immune system becomes. Will Paul

find the cure to his Tide Pod powers, or will he have to become the most unstoppable man alive at a life threatening level?
